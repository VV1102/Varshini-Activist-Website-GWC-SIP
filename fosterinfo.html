What is an Orphanage?

An institution that is set up for the care and education of orphaned children or children whose parents are deceased. This institution helps find fit guardians for these children.


What is a Foster Home

An institution that is set up for the care and education of foster children or children whose parents are unfit or unable to take care of their children. This institution helps find fit guardians for these children.

What Facilities to Orphanages and Foster Homes Generally Have?

Not all orphanages or foster homes are as bad as the ones in the movies or Oliver Twist. Although their quality varies, below are often some of the facilities offered at these homes.


  • Proper Clothing
  • Periodic Meals
  • Access to Education
  • Room and Boarding
  • Care from Wardens
  • Orphanage Officials Looking for Suitable Guardians
